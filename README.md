# Adidas-Sales-Dashboard-PowerBI
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Adidas sales performance with KPIs, drill-through pages and time-intelligence insights across products, cities, and employees.

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes Adidas sales data to provide clear business insights about sales performance, discounts, product categories, cities, and employee contribution.


## Key Features

- Total Sales & Sales After Discount KPIs
- Month-over-Month and Year-over-Year Sales Comparison
- Sales Over Time Analysis
- Sales by City Visualization
- Products per Category with Tooltip Insights
- Drill-through to Product Details Page
- Sales per Employee with Drill-through to Employee Details
- Insights Page (Top Products, Discount Impact)


## Skills Demonstrated

- Data Modeling in Power BI
- DAX Measures and Time Intelligence
- Interactive Dashboard Design
- Business Insight Visualization
